Refund, Return & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: 15 May 2026

We want you to love what you order. This policy explains when you can cancel an order, when we accept returns, and how refunds work. It is governed by the Consumer Protection Act, 2019 and the Consumer Protection (E-Commerce) Rules, 2020.

1. Cancellation by you

  • Standard (non-customised) products: you may cancel within 4 hours of placing the order, provided we have not dispatched it. Email us at brscustomgifts@gmail.com with your order reference.
  • Customised / personalised products (photo frames, photo mugs, name lockets, custom T-shirts, sashes, etc.): cancellation is allowed only if we have not yet started production. We typically begin production within a few hours, so please request cancellation immediately if needed.
  • Once production of a customised item has started, the order cannot be cancelled.

2. Cancellation by us

We may cancel your order — and refund the full amount — if:

  • the item is out of stock or unavailable;
  • there has been a pricing or description error;
  • we cannot deliver to your address;
  • we suspect fraud or a violation of our Terms;
  • the personalisation content you submitted is unlawful or infringes a third-party right.

3. Returns

We accept returns only in the following cases:

  • The product is damaged or defective on delivery.
  • The wrong item was shipped.
  • The item is materially different from what you ordered.

You must:

  1. Email us at brscustomgifts@gmail.com with your order reference and clear photos of the issue within 48 hours of delivery.
  2. Retain the original packaging until our team confirms how to proceed.
  3. If we approve the return, we will arrange pickup or share a return address. Please ship the item unused and in its original packaging.

4. What is NOT returnable

  • Personalised / customised products (unless damaged, defective, or wrong on delivery).
  • Perishable items.
  • Products that have been used, washed, altered, or are missing original tags / packaging.
  • Items reported after 48 hours from delivery.
  • Items damaged by misuse after delivery.

5. Refunds

  • Approved refunds are issued to the original payment method only.
  • Expected timelines:
    • UPI / wallet refunds: 1–3 business days.
    • Card refunds: 5–7 business days (subject to your bank).
    • Net-banking refunds: 3–7 business days.
  • The refund amount equals the price you paid for the affected item(s). Original shipping charges are not refunded unless the return is due to our error.
  • If you paid via the manual QR fallback (you submitted a PhonePe UTR reference), refunds will be sent back to the same UPI ID that paid us. Please share your UPI ID by email if it differs from the one used to pay.

6. Replacements

In cases of damaged / defective / wrong items, we may offer a replacement instead of a refund. The choice is yours.

7. Order not delivered

If a courier marks your order delivered but you have not received it, please email us within 24 hours. We will investigate with the courier; resolution may take up to 7 business days.

8. Grievance redressal

If you are unhappy with how we have handled a refund or return, please contact our Grievance Officer (see Privacy Policy). We will acknowledge within 24 hours and resolve within 15 days.
