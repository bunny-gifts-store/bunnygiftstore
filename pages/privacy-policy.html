2. Information we collect

Information you give us
  • Order checkout: name, email address, phone number, complete delivery address, pincode, items ordered, quantities, sizes.
  • Contact form: name, email, phone, message text.
  • Custom-print uploads (if applicable): photos or designs you provide for personalised products.
Information collected automatically
  • Server logs: IP address, browser user-agent, request timestamps. Used for security and abuse prevention. Retained up to 30 days.
  • Local browser storage: your shopping cart contents (stored on your device, not on our servers, until you check out).
Information from payment partners
  • PhonePe transaction reference, transaction state ("paid", "failed", "pending"). We do not see or store your card number, UPI PIN, or banking credentials — PhonePe handles those directly.

3. Why we collect it

  • To process and dispatch your order.
  • To communicate with you about your order, delivery, or refund.
  • To respond to messages you send through the contact form.
  • To prevent fraud, abuse, and to comply with legal obligations.
  • To improve the website (aggregate, non-identifying analysis only).

We rely on your consent (placing an order) and our legitimate interests (responding to messages, preventing fraud) as the legal basis under the DPDP Act.

4. Who we share it with

We do not sell your personal information. We share it only with the following processors, and only to the extent necessary:

  • PhonePe — for processing your payment.
  • Vercel (USA) — for hosting the website and APIs.
  • Supabase (USA / EU) — for storing order records.
  • Resend (USA) — for transactional email notifications to the store owner.
  • Google Sheets / Google Workspace (USA) — for our order tracking sheet.
  • Cloudflare (USA) — for bot protection.
  • Courier partners — for delivery (we share only your name, address, phone, and order details).
  • Government authorities — when required by law (e.g. court order, tax inspection).

Some of these processors store data outside India. Where this happens we have ensured contractual or technical safeguards consistent with the DPDP Act.

5. How long we keep it

  • Order data: 7 years (Indian tax and consumer-protection retention requirements).
  • Contact form messages: 2 years.
  • Server logs: 30 days.
  • Local cart storage: until you clear your browser data.

6. How we protect it

  • HTTPS / TLS on every page.
  • Database access restricted to server-side service keys only; the public website cannot read order data.
  • Admin access protected by strong password hashing (scrypt) and signed session cookies with short expiry.
  • Strict input validation and content-security policy to mitigate injection attacks.
  • No payment card data is ever stored by us.

No system is completely secure. If you discover a security issue, please email us at the address above.

7. Your rights under the DPDP Act

You have the right to:

  • access a summary of your personal data we hold;
  • request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data;
  • request erasure of your data (subject to legal retention obligations);
  • withdraw consent to processing where consent is the basis;
  • nominate another individual to exercise these rights on your behalf in the event of your death or incapacity;
  • grieve to our Grievance Officer (below); and
  • escalate to the Data Protection Board of India if your grievance is not addressed within 30 days.

To exercise any right, email us with the subject line "DPDP Request" and a brief description. We will respond within the timelines required by law.

8. Cookies

We use only first-party storage for your shopping cart and admin session. We do not use third-party advertising cookies. The Cloudflare Turnstile widget and Google Maps embed may set their own cookies subject to their respective privacy policies.

9. Children

The Site is intended for users aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect personal data of children. If you believe a child has provided us data, please contact us and we will delete it.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with a new "Last updated" date. Material changes will be communicated by email to recent customers where reasonably possible.
