Shipping & Delivery Policy

Last updated: 15 May 2026

1. Where we ship

We currently ship to addresses in Telangana and Andhra Pradesh only. If your pincode is outside these states, please contact us before placing an order — we may be able to make special arrangements.

2. Processing time

  • Standard (in-stock) products: dispatched within 1–2 business days after payment is confirmed.
  • Personalised / customised products (photo frames, photo mugs, name jewellery, T-shirts, sashes, resin art, etc.): production takes 2–5 business days, dispatched immediately after.
  • Large or bulk orders (gift hampers, corporate gifting): timelines confirmed by email after order placement.

3. Delivery time

After dispatch, estimated delivery time depends on destination:

  • Hyderabad & surrounding districts: 1–2 business days.
  • Other parts of Telangana: 2–3 business days.
  • Andhra Pradesh: 3–5 business days.

These are estimates. Courier delays, weather, festivals, or unforeseen events can extend them.

4. Shipping charges

Shipping charges depend on weight, destination, and the courier we use. They will be displayed (or communicated by email) before you confirm payment. We do not add hidden charges after order placement.

5. Tracking

Once dispatched, we will email and/or WhatsApp you the courier name and tracking number. If you do not receive tracking details within 3 business days of payment confirmation, please contact us.

6. Failed delivery / wrong address

  • If a delivery fails because the address you provided was incorrect or incomplete, or no one was available to receive the package after multiple attempts, we will contact you. Re-shipping may attract additional charges.
  • If a package is returned to us due to repeated failed delivery, we will issue a refund minus shipping and any return-courier charges.

7. Damaged in transit

Please inspect your package on delivery. If it is visibly damaged, take photos before opening and refuse delivery if possible. Email us within 48 hours with the photos and your order reference — see our Refund Policy for next steps.

8. Cash on delivery

We do not offer Cash on Delivery at this time. All orders are paid online before dispatch.
